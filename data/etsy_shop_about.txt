--- How Our Stickers Are Made ---

1. We monitor trending topics across the internet.
2. AI generates unique sticker designs based on those trends.
3. Each design is quality-checked for resolution, composition, and content safety.
4. Approved designs are printed on premium vinyl using professional-grade printers.
5. Every sticker is die-cut, waterproof, and UV-resistant.
